About DawaLens

DawaLens uses AI vision to help identify medicines from their physical appearance alone — no packaging needed. It is a safety companion, not a medical diagnosis tool.

Always confirm with a pharmacist or doctor before taking any medicine. DawaLens is a hackathon prototype built for Samsung Solve for Tomorrow 2026.
